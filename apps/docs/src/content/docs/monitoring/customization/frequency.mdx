---
title: Frequency
description: "How to change the frequency of your synthetic checks in OpenStatus"
---

By default, OpenStatus.dev will check your service every 30 minutes.

The min frequency options range from 10 minutes for free plans to 30 seconds for paid plans.

To change frequency of your monitors: _Select a monitor &rarr; Settings &rarr; Scheduling & Regions_.

We support the following frequencies:

- Every 30 seconds
- Every 1 minute
- Every 5 minutes
- Every 10 minutes
- Every 30 minutes
- Every 1 hour
